Information I Collect

When you submit the contact form on this website, I collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The subject of your message
  • The message content
  • Your preference regarding whether your message and name may be used in a YouTube video (via the checkbox on the form)

This information is provided voluntarily by you when you choose to submit the form.

How Your Information Is Used

I use the information you submit only for the following purposes:

  • To read and respond to your message
  • To follow up on your message if needed
  • If you give permission via the checkbox: I may show your message and the name you provided in a YouTube video (for example, in a Q&A)
  • If you do not give permission: Your message and name will not be used in any video

Your email address will never be shown publicly.
